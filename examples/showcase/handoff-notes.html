Risks

High
Enterprise email gateway delay. Some customers (Vercel, Notion) have email gateways that strip tokens or delay delivery past our 15-min TTL. Before 100% rollout, we need either a longer TTL for those tenants or a fallback to the legacy OAuth flow.
Medium
WebAuthn UX on Safari iOS. The platform-authenticator API is gated behind permissions some users decline by default. Need a clear fallback to TOTP for those cases.
Low
Session migration ambiguity. Existing OAuth sessions stay valid for 30 days. Edge cases around auth_v2-flag flip during an active session are tested but worth re-checking.
